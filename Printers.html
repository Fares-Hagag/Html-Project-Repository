Printers

A printer is a peripheral device that produces a physical copy or hard copy of the computerâ€™s output.

Two basic types:

  • Impact printer
  • Nonimpact printer

Impact Printer

  • An impact printer is a printer that has a print head that contacts the pepar to produce a character.
  • It uses ink ribbon.
  • It is noisy, produces Near-letter quality printouts, and is not commonly used today
  • Dot-matrix_ pins are used to make characters

Nonimpact printer

  • Inkjet printer: Also called bubble-jet; makes characters by inserting dots of ink onto paper.
  • Laser printer: works like a copier; quality determined by dots per inch (dpi).

Multifunction Printer

A multifunction printer combines the functions of a nonimpact printer, scanner, fax machine, and copier in one unit.

Plotter

  • A plotter is a printer that uses a pen that moves over a large revolving sheet of paper.
  • It is used in engineering and map making.
